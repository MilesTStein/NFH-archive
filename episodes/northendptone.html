North End Part 1

Having passed Faneuil Hall, we continue along the Freedom Trail through Boston's North End. The North End was split thematically - one episode focuses on race, culture, religion, how the neighborhood has changed, and how it has not. The other contains all the important stories that are left out of that thematic narrative. This episode is the latter. Featuring a taste-test of Brach's "Turkey Dinner" flavored candy corn.

About the Host

Miles Taylor is a tour guide on Boston's Freedom Trail. Officially he plays the Marquis de Lafayette for the Freedom Trail Foundation, but during his COVID-19 furlough he began doing weekly episodes for a variety show on Zoom, tackling about three sites at a time. These 30-ish minute segments have been aptly titled No Fucks History, and he is often introduced as 'Boston's No Fucks Historian', a title he finds highly amusing. Once the content on the Freedom Trail itself has been exhausted, he plans to choose a different moment in history at random to rant about each week. He may have a baby face but he is, in fact, 25, so all that wine he drinks on camera is perfectly legal. Shush.

The content on this website is in no way meant to reflect the tours provided by the Freedom Trail Foundation. Any similarities to the tour given by the Marquis de Lafayette are due to alcohol consumption. Relax, Suzanne.
